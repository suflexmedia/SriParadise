THE ENTERPRISE

Founded by Arjun Prakashh, Srii Paradiise is a global enterprise headquartered in Singapore, operating through multiple independent companies across 8 countries and 6 continents worldwide.

The group is built with a clear mission to create world-class hospitality and entertainment experiences while delivering long-term stakeholder value through disciplined leadership and trust.

Srii Paradiise is the principal investment holding company and promoter of global hospitality and entertainment projects. Each enterprise operates independently under its own leadership team, ensuring agility, accountability, and scaling on a global platform.

Srii Paradiise Hospitality

Boutique Hotels, Five-Star Hotels, Luxury Resorts, Casinos, Nightclubs, World-Class Speciality Restaurants-Lounges, and other Experiential Destinations.

Srii Paradiise International Studios (USA)

A Hollywood and Bollywood-anchored film and intellectual property platform spanning movie production, web series for multiple OTT platforms, international film distribution, and long-term content ownership with intellectual property rights preserved.
